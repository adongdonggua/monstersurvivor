How to Play

  • Desktop: Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character
  • Mobile: Use the virtual joystick on the left to control movement
  • Avoid enemy attacks while collecting power-ups
  • Survive as long as possible against endless waves
  • Adapt your strategy as monsters become stronger

About The Game

Monster Survivors is an action-packed roguelike where you battle endless waves of monsters. Choose your character, level up to unlock powerful abilities, and create unique builds for each run. With simple controls but deep gameplay mechanics, every playthrough offers a fresh challenge that will keep you coming back for more!
